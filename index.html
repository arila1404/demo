    HELLO !

    Hoaprox, a.k.a. dang ngoc quynh anh, is Vietnam’s #1 DJ & Producer ( The Champion Of The Remix New Generation 2017 ).

    At 14 years old he discovered his distinct personality and calling, when all his friends spent all their time with computer games, whereas Hoaprox used it to explore & learn about music.

    Hoaprox started producing music at 15 years old. Just two short years later Hoaprox stormed onto the music scene as a prodigy, greatly influencing and impacting Vietnam and Asia.

    Hoaprox has the ability to produce many diffrent electronic music genres, such as Progessive House , Future Bass, Trap, Bigroom, Bounce, Dubstep, Nudisco, Future House. Each song released embodies Hoaprox’s charisma and style.

    When performing on stage, He always shows off his passion in music, creativity and inspiration to the audience. With his mesmerizing performance style and mixing techniques, he masters the stage and drives the audience to bliss with his performance.
